Summary

I am a graduate student from Stony Brook University with a master's degree in Materials Science and Engineering. I am a passionate engineer by education and experience. My research interest and experience fall into areas of aerospace materials and its manufacturing, stress and failure analysis of materials, and general manufacturing industry. Being a materials scientist I have sound technical knowledge of thermal spray coatings, composite materials, their properties, and testing. I can work through the stress analysis of materials and their failure mechanisms fluently. I have a decent background in manufacturing, designing and testing.

Objective

Materials have tremendously transformed almost every sphere of our life; be it energy, communication, transportation or sports equipment. To meet the demanding needs for novel applications, such materials constantly challenge and push materials-science engineers & researchers to achieve newer heights. I want to be a part of this fraternity of Materials Science engineers and contribute my might in this world in however a small way I can.
