CVD EQUIPMENT CORPORATION

Technical Sales Associate

March 2017 - Present

CVD Materials Corporation; a subsidiary of CVD Equipment Corporation has acquired the assets of Tantaline A/S of Nordborg, Denmark who is a leading provider of tantalum coatings for corrosion resistance applications. Tantalum coatings are done through Chemical Vapor Deposition; a treatment know as Tantaline (R) which can be done on various intricate parts and geometries. The Tantalum coatings are highly corrosion resistant especially in hot corrosive acids where almost all other high-tech materials like Hastelloy C-276, Titanium, Zirconium, etc fail. Tantalum coated parts are used in wide variety of industries; for example in chemicals manufacturing, oil and gas, pharma, etc. I have joined CVD Materials Corporation as a Technical Sales/Marketing associate in functional vapor deposition technologies. Currently my duties include the following: New product development for corrosion resistant materials in manufacturing industries and other areas of business. Managing the technical market analysis/research in United States. Exploring new markets for such innovative technology and expanding the strategic partnership.
